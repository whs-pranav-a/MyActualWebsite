About Carbon Fiber Bikes

Pros of Carbon Fiber Bikes

Some pros include its lightweight frame, with low density, but a stronger frame. Another is the shock absorbtion of carbon fiber, containing a good level of rigidity. Lastly, when using carbon fiber, it can be easily molded to make multiple shapes and frames without difficulty.

Cons of Carbon Fiber Bikes

Some cons include its its shape, because one you choose a shape/size of a carbon fiber bike, you cannot remold or change the shape of the bike at a later time. Another is the aging of the resin, because the bike can possibly start whitening if you leave it out in the sun.
